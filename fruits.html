guanavana

Guanabana, or soursop, is a tropical fruit with a sweet and slightly tangy flavor. Native to the Americas, it comes from the Annona muricata tree. The green, spiny fruit has soft white flesh and is commonly used in juices, smoothies, and desserts. It is also known for its use in traditional medicine.

Pitaya

Pitaya, commonly known as Dragon Fruit, is a tropical fruit with a striking appearance, featuring bright pink or yellow skin and green scales. It has a mildly sweet flavor and a juicy, white or red flesh dotted with tiny black seeds. Native to Central America but now grown in many tropical and subtropical regions, pitaya is often enjoyed fresh, in smoothies, or as a decorative ingredient in salads and desserts.
